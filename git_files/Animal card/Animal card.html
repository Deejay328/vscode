Peregrine Falcon

The peregrine falcon is the fastest animal on the planet, diving at speeds of up to 250mph and catching prey mid-air.

  • Scientific name: Falco peregrinus
  • Average life span:13 years
  • Average wing span: 2.4-3.9 feet
  • Average length: 13-24 feet

The peregrine falcon is a solitary bird of prey. The adult has a white underside, black head, and grey beak.Historically known in North America as the duck hawk, they hunt medium-sized birds by swooping in on them in mid-air.
